About Christiana

A Journey of Faith and Redemption

My Testimony

I am Christiana Catherine White, a born-again Christian, wife, and author whose life has been transformed by the saving grace of Jesus Christ. My journey has been marked by challenges, trials, and moments when I felt lost and alone.

But in those darkest moments, I discovered the light of Christ—a light that doesn't just shine in the darkness but transforms it. Through His blood, I found redemption. Through His love, I found hope. Through His sacrifice, I found victory.

Born Again in Christ

Being born again isn't just a phrase to me—it's my reality. When I accepted Jesus Christ as my Lord and Savior, my entire world changed. The old things passed away, and all things became new. This transformation didn't happen overnight, but it happened through God's perfect timing and grace.

Every day, I walk in this newness of life, learning to trust Him more, to lean on His strength instead of my own, and to find peace in His presence even when the storms rage around me.

Through Hardship to Victory

Life has not been easy. I've faced hardships that seemed insurmountable, trials that tested my faith, and moments when I questioned everything. But in those moments, God showed Himself faithful. He never left me, never forsake me, and always provided a way through.

The victory I've experienced isn't because of my strength or wisdom—it's because of His blood that was shed on the Cross for me. It's because of His resurrection power that lives in me. It's because He is faithful to complete the work He started in me.

Sharing My Story

I wrote my first book because I believe that testimonies have power. When we share what God has done in our lives, we encourage others, build faith, and glorify His name. My hope is that my story will inspire others to seek God, trust Him in their darkest moments, and discover the victory that's available through Jesus Christ.

If you're reading this and you're going through a difficult time, know that you're not alone. God sees you, He loves you, and He has a plan for your life. Victory is possible in Christ, and His blood has already paid the price for your freedom, healing, and breakthrough.

"But thanks be to God, who gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ."

1 Corinthians 15:57 (NKJV)

My Mission

My mission is simple: to share the love of Christ, the power of His blood, and the hope of victory that's available to everyone who believes. Through my writing, my testimony, and my life, I strive to point others to Jesus—the Author and Finisher of our faith.

I believe that every believer has a testimony worth sharing, and every story of God's faithfulness has the power to change lives. My prayer is that God will use my story to draw others closer to Him and to reveal His saving power and unfailing love.

Pillars of My Faith

Salvation in Christ

I believe that salvation comes only through faith in Jesus Christ, who died on the cross for our sins and rose again, giving us eternal life.

The Word of God

The Bible is my foundation and guide. It is the living Word of God, breathed by the Holy Spirit, and profitable for doctrine, correction, and instruction in righteousness.

Victory Through Grace

Victory in life comes not through our own efforts, but through the grace of God and the power of the Holy Spirit working in and through us.

Connect With Me

I'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, need encouragement, or want to share your own testimony, please don't hesitate to reach out.

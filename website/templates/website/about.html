About Christiana

A Journey of Faith and Redemption

My Testimony

Christiana Catherine is born-again in Christ since September 2020. First debut book launches on 14th November 2025!

I am an author, devoted to Christ, whose life has been transformed by the saving grace of Jesus Christ. My journey has been marked by challenges, trials, and moments when feeling lost and alone.

However in those darkest moments, I discovered the light of Christ—a light and redemptive power that transforms. Through His blood, I found redemption. Through His love, I found hope. Through His sacrifice, I found victory.

I hope my story and ministry will help you receive healing and freedom in Christ.

Born Again in Christ

Being born again isn't just a phrase to me—it's my reality. When I accepted Jesus Christ as my Lord and Savior, my entire world changed. The old things passed away, and all things became new. This transformation didn't happen overnight, but it happened through God's perfect timing and grace.

Every day, I walk in this newness of life, learning to trust Him more, to lean on His strength instead of my own, and although storms raged around me, I found peace in His presence.

Through Hardship to Victory

Life has not been easy. I've faced hardships that seemed insurmountable, trials that tested my faith, and moments when I questioned everything. But in those moments, God showed Himself faithful. He never left me, never forsake me, and always provided a way through.

The victory I have found is in the blood of the cross and the truth of His word! The victory I found was finding Jesus and believing in myself to do the work of the Lord! All He is looking for is your yes! It is a partnership that He requires. It's because He is faithful to complete the work He started with me.

Sharing My Story

I wrote my first book because I believe that testimonies have power. I started with an empowerment sparked within me, gazing upon the end goal to eventually encourage others!

When we share what God has done in our lives, we edify others, build faith, and glorify His name. My hope is that my story will inspire others to seek God, trust Him in their darkest moments, and discover the victory that's available through Jesus Christ.

If you're reading this and you're going through a difficult time, know that you're not alone. God sees you, He loves you, and He has a plan for your life. Victory is possible in Christ, and His blood has already paid the price for your freedom, healing, and breakthrough. In the hope that when you read this story, hope will be restored, breakthrough will occur and joy will be overflowing in your life as you step into your triumph!

"But thanks be to God, who gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ."

1 Corinthians 15:57 (NKJV)

My Mission

My mission is simple: to share the love of Christ, the power of His blood, and the hope of victory that's available to everyone who believes. Through my writing, my testimony, and my life, I strive to point others to Jesus—the Author and Finisher of our faith.

I believe that every believer has a testimony worth sharing, and every story of God's faithfulness has the power to transform lives. My prayer is that God will use my story to draw others closer to Him and to reveal His saving power and unfailing love.

In hope that when that glorious day comes, of the bridegroom, we will enter heavens gates together.

Pillars of My Faith

Salvation in Christ

I believe that salvation comes only through faith in Jesus Christ, who died on the cross for our sins and rose again, giving us eternal life.

The Word of God

The Bible is my foundation and guide. It is the living Word of God, breathed by the Holy Spirit, and profitable for doctrine, correction, and instruction in righteousness.

Victory Through Grace

Victory in life comes not through our own efforts, but through the grace of God and the power of the Holy Spirit working in and through us.

Connect With Me

I'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, need encouragement, or want to share your own testimony, please don't hesitate to reach out.
